Review
Aftermath Less

Rich characters, complex plot, great pacing and a winning central character. I thoroughly recommend this read, no spoilers, it’s just a great book. Clever, with a lead character whom I can’t help but identify certain traits of my own with. It meant I cared about what happens to him and those around him and I wasn’t disappointed a single bit, at any point in this read. This is a writer with a clear gift for storytelling, weaving a tapestry that can sustain through many more adventures, I hope. I am delighted to have found this novel!
